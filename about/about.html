Professional Dress Standards

BUSINESS OFFICE STANDARD

(Regular day at work attire)

Men: Collared shirt or polo shirt, tie is optional. No denim pants regardless of color, no sneakers unless totally solid in color either black or white. Belt must be worn if pants have loops, and shirts must be tucked in and buttoned up completely--top button may be undone if no tie is worn.

Women: Slacks, skirt or dress (mid-length an minimum), blouse, closed toed shoes, may be loafers, flats, pumps ect...or solid colored athletic type shoes if worn with slacks. Hosiery should be worn if wearing a skirt or dress. Conservative yet appropriate accessories and jewelery is recommended.

BUSINESS FORMAL

(Interview attire)

Men: Suit, sportcoat & dresspants and shirt and tie are required. Dress shoes and dark colored socks ae required--no athletic type shoes may be worn. Loafers, wingtips and other dress-style shoes (having a glossy finish) are appropriate. Belts must be worn, suspenders may be worn in conjunction with the belt. Appropriate jewelery and accessories are recommended as long as they are conservative.

Women: Professional dress suits with either slacks or skirts (mid-length minimum) is required. Dress shoes, hosiery must be worn with a skirt or dress. Blouse should match the suit ensemble. Professional style office dresses may be worn as well. Conservative yet appropriate accessories and jewelery is recommended.
